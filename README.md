## 🚀 What I do

- **AI automation & integrations** — supplier intelligence, outreach pipelines, RFQ automation, and LLM-powered internal tools that replace manual back-office work.
- **Engineering & business web apps** — calculation engines, quoting tools, document/report generation (PDF/DOCX/XLSX/DXF), dashboards, and multi-role platforms.
- **High-conversion B2B websites** — fast, SEO-strong marketing sites with real lead funnels for manufacturers and industrial brands.
- **Agentic AI / MCP** — safety-governed agent tooling and custom MCP servers for operating real systems, not just chatting about them.

## 📌 Selected work

- **[oracle-dba-agentic-skills](https://github.com/Shreyas70773/oracle-dba-agentic-skills)** — a safety-first agentic Oracle DBA toolkit: 24 tier-gated operational domains, a custom Python MCP server, and an enforced autonomy model (read-only runs free, destructive work is human-gated). My deepest piece of AI-systems work.
- **[tank-pro-simulator](https://github.com/Shreyas70773/tank-pro-simulator)** (TankCalc Pro) — a full-stack TypeScript estimator and 3D simulator for process tanks, with a weight/cost engine and multi-format engineering reports (PDF/DOCX/XLSX/DXF + AutoCAD handoff).
- **Industrial client platforms** — websites and internal systems for manufacturers across Africa (industrial tanks, packaging machinery, supplier sourcing), built and shipped through [Pixel and Punch](https://pixelandpunch.com).
